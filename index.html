Namaste Nepal


This server's general purpose is to give people a simple, but unique environment, mainly for chatting. What this server offers is a chill vibe with everyone being a bit weird at times, but overall we like to treat each other as equals and have fun together in the process. We host all forms of community activities such as - podcasts, meme competitions, music events, fun games, movie nights and weekly giveaways.
